Group tour
See Tour
Description

Mendoza is a picturesque city highlighted by its traditional groves and an irrigation and water conduction system called ditches. It is a town with history and effort that was transforming the desert into a prosperous oasis. Our tours are developed in the departments located between vineyards visiting vineyards and olive groves. Also heading south of the province, it is possible to enjoy during the journey an important natural contrast of productive oases, such as the Uco Valley, deserts and water mirrors. The tour offers a varied range of colors and reliefs with historical sites and natural architectural works with thermal waters.

Mendoza is the land of the sun and good wine.

Experiences
Horseback riding
Description

The main purpose of the ride is to live with nature riding beautiful horses connecting with animals and native vegetation, leaving behind the noise of the city, being part of the landscapes of Mendoza and the culture of their places. The cavalcade is an activity for the whole family in harmony with nature and wildlife in its purest form.

Horseback riding in vineyards

The characteristics of soil, vines and irrigation in Mendoza are notorious. In the same route we see fruit crops, olive trees, and small orchards. Leaving the farm, we follow a path that takes us to the Mendoza River, always with its harmonious flow, allowing us to walk it and enjoy it as horse riders.

Horseback riding in high mountain

Program that takes place between 1,500 and 2,500 meters in height. During the tour we will know exclusively parts of a historical place. El Salto, where we will have the opportunity to observe panoramic views of the valley of Potrerillos, dike Potrerillos and the Cordón del Plata.

Optional

In any of the activities you can conclude with a roast beef, homemade bread, salads, red wine, mineral water, dessert and coffee.

It is an experience about the bond with animals and nature.
